About Us

About Maison Sara

Maison Sara was founded with a dream: to bring together high-quality, timeless pieces to accompany the most precious years of childhood.

Every brand in our store is carefully chosen for its design philosophy, production quality, materials and commitment to children’s health.

We bring distinguished brands from around the world to families, combining a trusted shopping experience with a warm and welcoming store.

Because we know childhood is not just about growing up — it is about discovering, learning and collecting beautiful memories.

Frequently Asked Questions

Good to Know

Where is Maison Sara?

Our store is located at Cumhuriyet Mahallesi 14041. Sokak No:43AB, Onikişubat / Kahramanmaraş. We are open Monday–Saturday, 10:00–19:00.

Which brands can I find?

We bring together the world’s beloved baby and kids brands under one roof, including Gap, Mini Melissa, Djeco, Melissa & Doug, Battat, Trixie, BIBS, Mimi & Lula, Classic World and Floss & Rock.

Do you sell toys?

Yes; our toy collection ranges from wooden toys and educational games to art activities and pretend play.

Which age groups do you cover?

From newborns to school age; a wide range spanning baby and kids clothing, shoes, feeding essentials, toys and accessories.

Can you help me choose a gift?

Of course; our team is happy to help you choose the right gift for any age and budget in store. You can also get suggestions over WhatsApp through our gift consulting service.

Do you sell online?

We currently serve only from our store in Onikişubat, Kahramanmaraş. You can browse our products on Instagram (@maisonsara.kids) and reach us with any questions.
